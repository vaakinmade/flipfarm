Overview
The most commonly raised poultry birds for commercial purposes are chickens.

A chicken that is raised for egg production is called a layer chicken, while the one that is raised for meat production is called a broiler chicken.

For prospective investors, commercial poultry farming can be very profitable with numerous gains and basic investment capital.

Commercial poultry farming businesses can also ensure high return of investment within a very short period, as some poultry birds like broiler chickens take a shorter duration of time to mature and do not require high maintenance - this helps with quick revenue generation.
